Our Commitment to Clean Water and Sanitation

Aqua Pure Network is dedicated to achieving the UN Sustainable Development Goal 6 (SDG 6): Clean Water and Sanitation. This goal aims to ensure availability and sustainable management of water and sanitation for all by 2030.

We focus specifically on the following aspects of SDG 6:

  • Goal 6.1: Safe and affordable drinking water - Ensuring universal access to safe and affordable drinking water.
  • Goal 6.2: Sanitation and hygiene - Achieving access to adequate and equitable sanitation and hygiene for all and ending open defecation.
  • Goal 6.3: Water quality - Improving water quality by reducing pollution, eliminating dumping, and minimizing the release of hazardous chemicals and materials.
  • Goal 6.4: Water-use efficiency - Increasing water-use efficiency across all sectors and ensuring sustainable withdrawals and supply of freshwater.
  • Goal 6.5: Integrated water resources management - Implementing integrated water resources management at all levels, including transboundary cooperation.

By aligning our strategies and operations with these goals, we aim to make a significant impact on global water sustainability and drive positive change within our community and beyond. Our initiatives include building sustainable water infrastructure, promoting water conservation practices, and advocating for policies that support clean water and sanitation for all.
